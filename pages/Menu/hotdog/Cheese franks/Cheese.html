  • Whether you choose cheddar or cream, a bit of cheese can really change the flavour of your hot dog and give it a creamy, gooey texture. It goes best with chili, or some sweet grilled onions. From an overall perspective, the hamburger is a better option. A 4-ounce hamburger has about six times the amount of protein as a hot dog, with about a quarter of the sodium.
